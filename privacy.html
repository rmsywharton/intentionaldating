Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

Firesign Holdings LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website and pre-launch platform for The Intentional Dating App at intentionaldating.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you interact with our platform.

By submitting your information through our founding member application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

At this pre-launch stage, we collect only information you voluntarily provide, which may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional at application stage)
  • City and geographic location
  • Responses to application questions regarding dating intentions, relationship goals, and conflict resolution style
  • Communication preferences including SMS and newsletter opt-in choices
  • Referral codes generated at submission

We do not collect payment information at this stage. No charges are made during the waitlist or founding member application process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected strictly to:

  • Review, evaluate, and manage your placement in the Cohort Alpha founding member selection or city founding list
  • Send operational communications including application status updates, beta testing invitations, and launch announcements
  • Send SMS launch alerts for your city, if you opted in
  • Send the Intentional newsletter including dating insights, relationship content, and app updates, if you opted in
  • Analyze application responses in aggregate to improve product development and community design
  • Track referral activity associated with your unique referral code

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our landing page may use basic operational cookies or analytics tools to measure website traffic, form completion rates, and user experience optimization. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can manage or disable cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard encryption protocols through our infrastructure providers, which include Cloudflare Pages and Google Workspace services. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We retain your data until the official launch of the application, until you request deletion, or until you unsubscribe from all communications, whichever comes first.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, DNS, and security infrastructure
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Google Workspace — application data logging and storage

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admissions@intentionaldating.app.

7. Children's Privacy

The Intentional Dating App is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has submitted information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our platform evolves. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
